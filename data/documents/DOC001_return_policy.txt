Title: Return Policy
Date: 2024-01-15

Our return policy allows customers to return most items within 30 days of purchase.
Items must be in original condition, unused, and in original packaging.
Electronics must be returned within 15 days and must include all original accessories.
Perishable goods, digital downloads, and customized products are non-returnable.
To initiate a return, customers must contact support@opsstore.com with their order ID.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after the returned item is received.
Shipping costs for returns are borne by the customer unless the item is defective.
