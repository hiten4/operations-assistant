Title: Product Notes - Laptops
Date: 2024-02-01

Our flagship laptop model (SKU: LAP-PRO-15) has a 15-inch display with 4K resolution.
Battery life averages 10 hours under normal use conditions.
The laptop supports up to 64GB RAM and 2TB SSD storage configurations.
A known firmware issue with models manufactured before October 2023 causes occasional Wi-Fi drops.
Affected units can be identified by serial numbers starting with "LP23A".
A firmware patch (v2.1.4) is available on our support portal and resolves this issue.
Customers with defective units are eligible for an extended 90-day return window.
