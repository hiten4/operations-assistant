Title: Shipping Policy
Date: 2024-01-15

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days and costs $4.99 for orders under $50.
Orders above $50 qualify for free standard shipping automatically.
Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available for $12.99.
Overnight shipping is available for $24.99 on orders placed before 2 PM EST.
We ship to all 50 US states and internationally to 35 countries.
International orders may incur customs duties not covered by us.
Tracking numbers are emailed within 24 hours of dispatch.
Deliveries to PO boxes are only supported via standard shipping.
