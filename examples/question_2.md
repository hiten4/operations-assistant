### Finding 1: Electronics Return Window
Electronics must be returned within 15 days of purchase (compared to 30 days for
standard items) and must include all original accessories. (Source: DOC001)

### Finding 2: Defective Laptop Exception

manufacture) are affected by a firmware Wi-Fi issue. These units qualify for an
extended 90-day return window. (Source: DOC003)

### Finding 3: Return Initiation
To initiate any return, customers must email support@opsstore.com with their order ID.
Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after receipt. (Source: DOC001)

## Recommendations

1. Inform electronics customers of the 15-day window at point of purchase.
2. For LP23A serial laptops: proactively offer the firmware patch (v2.1.4) before
   the return window expires to reduce avoidable returns. (Source: DOC003)
